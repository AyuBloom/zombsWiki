# Blank Wave

## Phenomenon
When you reached wave 14 while being alone inside your party, no zombie would spawn at that wave. Next wave zombies spawned like normal.

Zombies spawned if your party had 2 or more players.

## Cause
Unknown. Probably an oversight in the code.
