# Weird Zombie Spawning

## Phenomenon
Normally, the zombs.io zombies should spawn at a relatively constant distance from the stash. But for whatever reason, a group of one or more zombies will spawn closer than normal, even inside your base!

## Cause

Unknown.

## Examples

### 18th April 2021
A Discord user named LOLOL sent an image showing a Zombie that spawned INSIDE his base, on a bunch of doors.
